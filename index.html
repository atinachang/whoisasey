who is asey?

My name is Atina, also known as ASEY (AC) and I am a Front-End Developer and DJ born and raised in Toronto.


I love combining the worlds of logic and endless possibilities to create exciting environments, whether it be on the web or in the club.


As a youth, I found myself on Neopets coding my online store, and didn't think it would amount to anything in the future. It wasn't until recently that I found myself exploring the great world of code!


You can find me playing in various bars across Downtown Toronto.


I have a biweekly radio show on ISO Radio, a community online radio station in Toronto. Find out more here
